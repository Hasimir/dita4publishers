f-stop

A value that indicates the size of the lens aperture. The aperture is the size of the opening in the lens that allows light to enter the camera and expose the image sensor. A small aperture is represented by a large number; a large aperture by a small number. f/2.8 (or F2.8) indicates a large aperture (opening) and lets in more light. f/16 (or F16) indicates a small aperture (opening) and lets in less light The typical values in between these are: F4, F5.6, F8, and F11.
