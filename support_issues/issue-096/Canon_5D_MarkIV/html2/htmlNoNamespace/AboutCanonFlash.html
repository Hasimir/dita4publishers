About Canon Flash

The Canon flash system is the most advanced system on the market. Use it to get perfect exposures both indoors and outdoors.

Your camera includes a built-in flash and a hot shoe for mounting an external flash, like the Canon 580 EX II. External flashes let you get the flash off-axis with the lens for more interesting photos and to avoid red-eye. Wireless triggering enables you to control multiple off-camera flashes.
