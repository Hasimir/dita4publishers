d4p_mediaDomain

The d4p_mediaDomain defines specializations and markup for the inclusion of interactive media (assets that are not graphics) into publications, such media may be video or audio. It is modeled as closely as possible on the HTML5 video and related elements. Output support is provided for HTML, HTML5, and EPUB.
