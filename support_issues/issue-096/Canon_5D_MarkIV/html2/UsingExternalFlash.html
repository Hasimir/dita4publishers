Using External Flash

Using one or more external flashes can produce stunning lighting effects.

Your camera is compatible with all Canon EX external flashes, such as the and with the ST-2 flash controller.

To use a single external on-camera flash:

  1. Ensure that both flash unit and camera are turned off.

  2. Mount the external flash in the hot shoe and lock it into place.

  3. Turn on both camera and flash.

  4. Rotate the flash head or tilt it up to bounce the light from the flash off a wall or the ceiling.

  5. Focus and shoot. After each shot, wait until the flash cycles.
