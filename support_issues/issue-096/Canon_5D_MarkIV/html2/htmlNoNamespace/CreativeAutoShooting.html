Creative Auto Shooting

The Creative Auto (CA) mode enables you to exercise more control over the decisions the camera makes when shooting in Intelligent Auto.

In the CA mode you can easily change the depth of field and drive mode. You can also choose the a ambience you want to convey in your images. The default settings are the same as the Intelligent Auto mode.1
