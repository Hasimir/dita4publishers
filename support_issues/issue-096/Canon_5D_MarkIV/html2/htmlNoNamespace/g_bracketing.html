bracketing

Taking a series of photographs of the same subject at different exposures to insure the "correct" exposure; useful when shooting in situations where a normal metering reading is difficult to obtain. Taking additional pictures of the subject through a range of exposures-both lighter and darker-when unsure of the correct exposure. The 5D Mark IV has automatic bracketing, while manually you can bracket by the use of, say, adjusting apertures or shuttle speeds setting or both, manually adjusting ISO setting, or even adjusting the flash output power etc.
