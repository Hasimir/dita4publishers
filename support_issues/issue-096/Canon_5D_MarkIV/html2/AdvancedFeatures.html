Chapter 6. Advanced Features

After you have adjusted to the basic controls and settings of your camera, consider these advanced features.

Topics:

  • Shooting RAW vs. JPEG

  • Understanding Metering Modes

  • Shooting RAW vs. JPEG

    Shooting in RAW format enables you to produce better results if you post-process your images. Shooting in JPEG produces small files and requires no post-processing.
  • Understanding Metering Modes

    Metering modes enable you to choose the area of the image that the camera measures to calculate the correct exposure.
