Lists

This topics contains samples of all the different kinds of base lists (ol, ul, sl, dl) in various combinations. One challenge with rendering lists in DITA is that list items are not required to contain paragraph elements, so a list item may consist of just PCDATA or PCDATA followed by paragraphs or other paragraph-level elements or may have only paragraphs.
